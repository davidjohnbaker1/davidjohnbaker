Workshops

I offer tranings and workshops on various topics on topics related to data science and am open to designing bespoke materials in order to suit your needs. Recently I become a certified RStudio Instructor, have worked as a Lead Instructor of Data Science at Flatiron School, and have also designed and delivered workshops for Minerva Statistical Consulting. I specialize in teaching beginners with little to no experience in data science.
